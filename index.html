The heart behind Golden Touch

It began the day I walked into my grandmother's home — and she fell into my arms and cried. No one should ever feel alone or unsafe in their own home.

After my grandfather passed, I traveled to help my grandmother — living alone, far from family, after losing her partner of 47 years. Her home had become too much to manage. I saw how something as simple as a clean, comfortable space can restore dignity, independence, and peace of mind. That's why Golden Touch exists.
